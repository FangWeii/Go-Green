West Africa

The purpose is to empower the children of Mali by expanding educational opportunities for them within their own communities. The inspiration and vision came, in part, through working with families to define and solve the problems they face on a daily basis and to better their standard of living.

East Africa

We support local projects that target the needs of disadvantaged children in East Africa. We support small projects in Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda that make a real difference. We are helping centers, education programmes, individual pupils and projects for communities struggling to take care of their children.

India

Although there is more wealth in India than ever before, it hasn’t fully translated into healthcare, education or better nutrition for the poor. We are bringing healthcare to the impoverished areas of India, and we’re pioneering new ways of reaching communities to help treat malnutrition and prevent deaths.

Latin America

We work in Latin America to provide for some of the world’s poorest children. With a population of 568 million, nearly 25% live on less than $2 a day. We work in these areas to help bring hope and possibilities into children’s lives, allowing them to thrive in the bright futures they all deserve.
